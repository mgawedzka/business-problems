Business Problems

What’s the purpose of IT in modern business?

The role of IT is modern business is to improve business processes, productivity, and profitability. Having your business online can improve competitiveness and drive revenue growth. Many customers look for products online, therefore, it is very important to have an effective online presence including marketing and online shopping websites. Communication with customers is key for business and modern online communication can help to provide instant customer support. Business growth is achieved through modern technology; for example, higher national and/or global sales. Online banking transactions make it possible to serve customers globally.

What causes digital projects to fail?

Digital projects can fail when they don't account for business processes, don't secure all executive buy-in. Sometimes businesses can invest in shiny objects new technology that has no customer value. The other reason why a digital project can fail is a poor definition and expectation settings.

How does GDPR impact digital projects?

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Businesses collect customer data to help design business strategy. GDPR compliance is important for businesses to retain customer information such as banking information, addresses, contacts, etc. There are high penalties for companies who fail to comply with GDPR. Many companies have to delete customer data because they can not achieve GDPR compliance; it can be difficult and expensive.

How would you go about testing digital solutions?

I would check that all functions, buttons work on every screen. I would do a general code review. The best practice is to conduct more software testing during development phase to save time and money. I would perform a static code analysis, unit testing, and single user performace testing.

How do copyright and other constraints affect websites?

Copyright stops other people from copying your work, distribuiting it, publishing, performing. It can be copied for private use only. Your original work is automatically protected when you create a new artistic work, software, a recording or a written work. Website contents including images are protected with copyright. You can include the copyright symbol © on your website; however, it is not necessary. There are many websites offering Royalty Free images, music, art, which are not subject to copyright, for example wwww.unsplash.com. Websites can have age restricted access. The UK is the first country in the world to verify age of the user for online pornography since 15 July 2019.

How can we overcome issues people with disabilities may face when using webpages?

Use alt tags to describe an image on a website, which are read aloud by a screen reader for people with a visual impairment. In addition, you should put periods in abbreviations to make it readeable by the screen reader, for example D.W.P. for Department of Work and Pensions. You can create subtitles and transcripts for videos using youtube tools. Links embedded on the website should be clearly described, insted of just saying click-here for more information. It is useful to underline your links using contrasting colors to make the link visible for colorblind web-users. The choice of colors can make website difficult to read for colorblind users who can't see yellow, green, and blue. For example, black text on a dark blue background will be hard to read. The best practice is to use black text on a white background.
